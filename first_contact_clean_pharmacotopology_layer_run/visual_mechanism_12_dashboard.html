Boundary Rules

Contact Maps Are The First Proof Target
Scoring starts with coarse native-contact overlap, not a global fold label.
Trajectory Is Coarse-Grained
The path is a visual reconstruction of candidate closure, not atomistic dynamics.
Native Contacts Are Used Only After Prediction
Prediction is sequence-only; locked contact targets enter only during scoring and rendering.
Failures Are Visual Evidence
Low overlap and overclosure are kept visible instead of hidden behind a single label.
Global Folding Claim Remains Locked
The workbench refuses solved-folding, clinical, molecule, and protein-design claims.
