# V38 Blind Mechanism Generalization Panel Protocol

V38 tests whether the V37 mechanism grammar survives blind target-name masking
and near-decoy pressure. It is still not structure prediction, MD, or a
folding-solved claim.

The panel has nine masked targets:

- Three known positives from V36/V37.
- Six near-decoys: two membrane/transport contrasts, two chemokine/small
  soluble contrasts, and two folded non-IDP contrasts.

The assignment function reads masked dossiers only. The answer key is stored
separately and is used only after assignment for evaluation.

Passage requires recovery of all three known positives, no hard-grammar false
positives among decoys, target-name masking, unmasked/masked consistency,
swapped-dossier detection, and leakage controls.
