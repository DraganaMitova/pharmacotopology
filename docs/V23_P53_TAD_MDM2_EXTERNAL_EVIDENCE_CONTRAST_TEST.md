V23 is a zero-MD external-evidence contrast test. It consumes the V22 acquisition panel and the V18/V18b p53 pressure-context results.

It checks that the external disorder-source context supports an isolated p53 TAD abstain guard, while the 1YCR partner-bound p53/MDM2 complex preserves partner-induced interface/helix role evidence.

This is not a folding claim.

Required invariants:

- `claim_allowed = false`
- `positive_folding_evidence_found = false`
- `new_md_executed = false`
- `fixed_residue_cutoff_used = false`
- `native_metrics_used_for_selection = false`
- no autonomous isolated-TAD compact-fold claim
- no partner interface promoted into a universal p53 fold claim

External couplings are not required for this first V23 contrast test. They remain missing/optional for stronger future evidence tests.
