# V21 Pressure Evidence Panel Summary

V21 summarizes the zero-MD pressure-context evidence panel after V18/V18b, V19, and V20.

It is not a folding claim. It locks the distinction between pressure evidence and folding evidence:

- p53/MDM2: disorder / partner-induced pressure evidence.
- KcsA: membrane / pore / environment pressure evidence.
- XCL1: metamorphic / state-specific pressure evidence.

Forbidden in V21:

- new MD or membrane MD,
- target-specific threshold tuning,
- fixed residue cutoff as decision law,
- native-metric selection,
- whole-fold / universal folding claim.

The expected locked status is `V21_PRESSURE_EVIDENCE_PANEL_SUMMARY_LOCKED` with `positive_folding_evidence_targets = []` and `claim_allowed = false`.
MD is not recommended next; missing external evidence and annotations should be acquired first.
